About Sundog Roasting Co.


What's with the name?

Sundog Coffee got it's name from a rare weather phenomenon.
It creates an optical illusion where the sun seems to have doubled, or even tripled, with mock suns flanking the real one. These fake are said to "dog" the sun. Hence the name sun dogs.
So why is this relevant?
The owner and founder, Allison Barnett, has fond memories of the beautiful atmospheric phenomenon. She spent several months living in Patagonia while teaching English. Most mornings she'd enjoy a cup of coffee at dawn while watching the sun rise.
It was during this time she developed an appreciation for the sun dogs and coffee. So when she decided to start her own coffee company after moving back to the states, she knew exactly what to name it.
Allison's greatest passion is traveling, of which she still does often to find delicious new coffee beans. Her business has taken her all over the world, from Kenya to Hawaii. She hopes to create the same magic for you that she used to feel watching the sun dogs over the Andes. Her goal is to create the perfect cup of coffee for every person; and for every palette.
